Official Rules, Policies & Guidelines 2026

1. Registration and Participation Policies

  • Every participant must complete the official registration form with accurate details, including the student's full name, father's name, and mother's contact number.
  • Participation fee must be paid successfully, and the payment status must be verified before competing in any category (LP, UP, High School, HSS).
  • Participants must carry their digital ID card or registration confirmation certificate to the venue at all times.

2. Scoring, Grading & Evaluation Criteria

  • The total score is calculated based on Kalolsavam performances, Quiz marks, Improvement tests, and Supplementary (Re-Exam) scores where applicable.
  • Full A+ Criteria: To achieve a "Full A+ Grade", a student must secure at least **90%** of the maximum total marks.
  • Grading Structure:
    • 90% and above: A+ (Full A+ Achieved / High Distinction)
    • 80% to 89%: A (Passed)
    • 70% to 79%: B+ (Passed)
    • 60% to 69%: B (Passed)
    • 40% to 59%: C (Passed)
    • Below 40% (Less than 30 marks total): Requires Re-Exam / Improvement.

3. Improvement and Supplementary Exam (Re-Exam) Policies

Important Rule for Low Scorers:
  • Students who score less than 30 marks in their initial evaluation are strictly required to take the Supplementary Exam (Re-Exam).
  • The Re-Exam consists of exactly **80 mandatory questions** testing technical and academic knowledge.
  • Final certification and pass status will only be officially locked and issued *after* including the improvement and Re-Exam scores.

4. Code of Conduct & Digital Certification

  • All participants must maintain discipline and ethical standards throughout the cultural and technical events.
  • Digital certificates generated through the portal contain unique ID credentials, school signatures, and verified performance records.
  • Any form of malpractice or manipulation of scores will result in immediate disqualification from the academy portal.
